QUERIES:- 

1) Query to retrieve all employees with a salary greater than 30,000 
Code:- 
SELECT * FROM Employee 
WHERE Salary > 30000;

2)Query to get the list of suppliers who supply 8Rice9 
Code:- 
SELECT * FROM Supplier 
WHERE Product_name = 'Rice'; 

3)Query to find the total stock quantity of all products 
Code:- 
SELECT SUM(Stock_Quantity_in_quintal) AS Total_Stock 
FROM Product;

4)Query to retrieve all customers living in 8Shimoga9 
Code:- 
SELECT * FROM Customer

IRA9

6)Query to find employees who have 8Assistant Director9 as their designation

7)Query to get the product details for 8Wheat9 
Code:-

8)Query to get the address and phone number of suppliers for 8Sugar9 
Code:- 
SELECT Supplier.FName, Supplier.LName, 
Supplier.Ration_shop_in_smg, Inventory.Phone_no 
FROM Supplier 
JOIN Product ON Supplier.Supplier_id = Product.Supplier_id 
JOIN Inventory ON Product.Product_Name = 
Inventory.Product_name 
WHERE Product.Product_Name = 'Sugar';

9)Query to list all employees born in 1990
